Our Story

About Sinha Overseas India

Sinha Overseas India was founded by two brothers, Tanay Sinha and Pranay Sinha, driven by a shared vision and unwavering dedication. Although the company was initially established in 2019, it became fully operational in 2024.

We are an international logistics, freight forwarding, and supply chain service provider committed to delivering reliable and efficient solutions. Our team continuously strives to expand our capabilities and services to meet and exceed our clients' logistics needs.

Our Comprehensive Services

We offer a full spectrum of logistics services designed to meet all your shipping needs, ensuring your cargo reaches its destination safely and on schedule.

Ocean Freight

Reliable and cost-effective sea cargo solutions for shipments of all sizes.

Air Freight

Customs & Port Clearance

Hassle-free import/export clearance with full compliance and expert guidance.

Project & OOG Cargo

Transportation
